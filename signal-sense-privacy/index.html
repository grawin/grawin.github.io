Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Signal Sense ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Signal Sense accesses and displays technical data directly from your device's modem and hardware to provide signal monitoring services:

  • Precise Location: Required to map signal strength and identify nearby cell towers/Wi-Fi networks.
  • Cellular Metrics: Signal strength (RSRP, RSRQ, RSSI), carrier information, and cell identifiers (PCI, TAC, CID).
  • Wi-Fi Metrics: SSID, BSSID, frequency, and congestion levels.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data regarding how the app is used (e.g., screens viewed, feature interactions).
  • Performance Data: Technical metrics such as app start-up time and network latency to help us optimize the app.

How We Use Information

  • Local Display: Signal and location data are primarily processed and stored locally on your device for real-time visualization and history logs.
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage and performance data help us identify bugs and improve the user experience.
  • Crash Reporting: We use anonymized reports to identify and fix technical issues.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase to improve app stability and performance. The following services are included:

  • Firebase Analytics: Helps us understand how users interact with the app.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Provides anonymized crash reports.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: Monitors app responsiveness.
Your Choice: You can opt-out of Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance data collection at any time by going to Settings > Privacy & Diagnostics within the app and toggling off "Usage Analytics & Crash Reports."

Data Security

We do not sell your data. Signal logs and location history are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers. Only anonymized technical metrics mentioned in the "Third-Party Services" section are shared with Google Firebase.
